


This Android application calculates your BMI based on your weight and height inputs.


Features:

Calculate BMI: Input your weight and height, then click "Calculate" to see your BMI.
BMI Category: Your BMI is categorized as underweight, normal weight, overweight, or obese.
Navigation: Easily switch between inputting your data and viewing your BMI.


Usage:

Input Screen: Enter your weight and height, then click "Calculate".
Result Screen: View your BMI and its category. Click "Back to Input" to input new data


Tools used:

The instructions provided in moodle
